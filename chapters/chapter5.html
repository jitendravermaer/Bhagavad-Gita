Krishna explains the difference and unity between renunciation and selfless action. He teaches that both paths lead to liberation when practiced with detachment and wisdom, but selfless action is easier for most.

In Chapter 5, Krishna clarifies the confusion between the path of renunciation and the path of selfless action. He emphasizes that both, when practiced with detachment, lead to the same goal. However, selfless action is more practical for most seekers.
